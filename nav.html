My Work

We offer our expecting mothers antenatal (before birth) and postnatal (after birth) care including the postnatal check six weeks after delivery. It involves treatments and trainings to ensure a healthy pre-pregnancy, pregnancy, and birthing process for the mother and for her child. During each antenatal visit, we will check that you and your baby are well, give you useful information about being pregnant and answer any questions you may have. An important part of antenatal care is getting information that will help you to make informed choices about your pregnancy. Please note that all ultrasound 4D scans are charged and call-outs for all ultrasound scans must be paid for. Standard 2D ultrasound remains covered under membership. Antenatal classes are available, please ask nursing staff for information
